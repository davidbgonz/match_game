Match Game

Game Rules

  • The objective of the game is to find all the pairs in the least moves
  • Click on any two cards.
  • If the two images match, you got a pair.
  • If they don't match, they will be turned face down.
  • Remember what was on each card and where it was.
  • The game is over when all the cards have been matched.
  • The player with least moves will be the highest score.
  • Every time you start a new game, a random selection of the cards ensures a different game
